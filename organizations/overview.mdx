---
title: "Organizations"
description: "Organizations group your connections, saved queries, and team members in Dory."
---

An organization is the top-level workspace in Dory. Everything you work with — connections, saved queries, and team members — belongs to an organization. You can be a member of multiple organizations and switch between them at any time.

## Creating your first organization

When you sign in for the first time, Dory prompts you to create an organization before you can access the workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter a name">
    Go to the **Create organization** screen. Type a name for your organization — for example, `Acme Data Team` or `My workspace`.

    Dory automatically generates a URL slug from the name you enter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the organization">
    Click **Create organization**. Dory creates the organization, sets you as its owner, and redirects you to the connections page at `/{slug}/connections`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can invite team members after the organization is created. You do not need to add anyone during setup.
</Tip>

## Organization settings

Open **Settings** from the sidebar, then select **Organization** to manage organization details.

### Name

The display name shown in the app. It does not affect your workspace URL.

### Slug

The slug appears in every URL within your workspace — for example, `https://app.getdory.dev/acme/connections`. Slug rules:

- Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only
- No spaces or special characters
- Must be unique across all organizations

<Warning>
  Changing the slug immediately redirects you to the new URL. Any bookmarks or shared links using the old slug will stop working.
</Warning>

Click **Save changes** to apply updates to the name or slug. Only members with the **owner** role can update organization settings.

## Connections and saved queries

All connections and saved queries are scoped to the organization they were created in. They are not shared across organizations.

When you switch to a different organization, the sidebar and workspace reflect only the connections and saved queries belonging to that organization.

## Switching between organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations, use the organization switcher in the sidebar to move between them. Selecting an organization sets it as your active workspace and updates the URL to `/{slug}/...`.

## Creating additional organizations

You can create more than one organization from the organization switcher.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the organization switcher">
    Click your current organization name in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new organization">
    Select **Create organization** from the menu. Enter a name and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start working">
    Dory switches to the new organization and takes you to its connections page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each organization starts empty. Connections and saved queries from other organizations are not copied over.
</Note>
