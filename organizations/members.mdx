---
title: "Members & invitations"
description: "Invite team members to your organization and manage their access."
---

Organization members share access to connections, saved queries, and workspace features. You manage membership from **Settings > Organization**, which lists all current members and pending invitations.

## Roles

Every member has one of four roles. Roles control what actions a member can take within the organization.

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **owner** | Full control — can update organization settings, manage members, manage invitations, and manage billing. |
| **admin** | Can manage members and invitations, read and write connections and workspaces. |
| **member** | Can read and write connections and workspaces. Cannot manage other members. |
| **viewer** | Read-only access to connections and workspaces. |

<Note>
  Only members with the **owner** role can update the organization name or slug, and manage billing.
</Note>

## Inviting members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open organization settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Organization** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the invitations section">
    Scroll to the **Members** or invitations panel on the organization settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter an email address and role">
    Type the invitee's email address and select the role they should receive — `owner`, `admin`, `member`, or `viewer`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Invite**. Dory sends an email invitation to the address you entered. The invitation appears in the pending invitations list until the recipient accepts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Invitations have an expiry date. If the recipient does not accept before it expires, you will need to send a new invitation.
</Info>

## Accepting an invitation

When someone invites you to an organization, you receive an email with a link to accept. Clicking the link takes you to Dory, where you can accept or reject the invitation.

Once accepted, the organization appears in your organization switcher and you can access it immediately.

## Updating a member's role

Owners and admins can change the role of an existing member.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open organization settings">
    Go to **Settings > Organization** and find the member in the members list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the role">
    Select a new role from the role dropdown next to the member's name and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a member

Only members with permission to delete members (owners and admins) can remove someone from the organization.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open organization settings">
    Go to **Settings > Organization** and locate the member you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the member">
    Click the remove action next to their name and confirm. The member immediately loses access to the organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Removing a member cannot be undone. To restore access, you must send a new invitation.
</Warning>

## Re-sending or canceling an invitation

Pending invitations are listed in the organization settings page.

- To **cancel** an invitation, click the cancel action next to the pending invitation. The invite link in the recipient's email becomes invalid immediately.
- To **re-send** an invitation, cancel the existing one and create a new invitation to the same email address.
